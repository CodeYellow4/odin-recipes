Savoury Vegan Bolognese

Photo by Joice Kelly on Unsplash

Description

A classic and savoury vegan bolognese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 200 g vegan mince of your choice
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 thick slice of cellery root
  • 5 brown mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 100 ml of red wine
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • fresh basil, oregano, thyme and rosemary to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the onions and garlic cloves in small pieces.
  2. Heat up the oil in a pan. Add the onions and garlic. Braise lightly until transparent.
  3. Add vegan mince and sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Chop carrot, cellery and mushrooms in fine pieces. Add them to the pan and sauté everything for another 3 minutes.
  5. Add tomato paste and red wine. Stir well to avoid clumps of tomato paste.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and let it all simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. In the meantime, cook your favourite pasta.
  8. Chop herbs finely and add them to the bolognese as you please.
  9. Finally, add salt and pepper to taste.

Now it's time to arrange your pasta and savoury bolognese on a plate. If you like, feel free to add grated vegan cheese. Enjoy!
